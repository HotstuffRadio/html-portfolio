The Best Movies According to Johnny

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time.

Blade Runner (1982)

This is my favourite movie of all time [*possibly] if not for the dystopian sci-fi atmosphere then for the amazing sci-fi dystopian soundtrack by Vangelis

Last Days (2005)

Gus Van Sant tackles this beautiful and tragically sad sojourn of the last days of Kurt Cobains life with an amazing sumptuos score by Hildergard Westerkamp

Jaws (1975)

As far as American allegorical metaphorical block busters go - this is the daddy! - Watch out the shark *Trump is coming again!
