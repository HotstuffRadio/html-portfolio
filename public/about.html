Meet Johnny, the coder behind the mic! With a knack for crafting captivating narratives, Johnny is not just a podcast and code producer, theyre an absolute mess of skills; from the airwaves of SoundArt radio in Totnes UK to the ancient hills of Athens, Johnny is weaving some weird magic in their journey through audio, code and image. Each project reflects a mosaic of multi media magic through the lens of global experiences accumulated over many years in the sonic visual realm.

Johnny loves exploring the frontiers of sound, image and text. Max Msp, Adobe Suite, Analog studio bits, Binaural microphones are the tools used to infuse their work with an innovative edge that blurs conventional notions of art with activism. This ethos produces dynamic work that delves deep into the world of art disruption, offering fresh perspectives and insights with every project
